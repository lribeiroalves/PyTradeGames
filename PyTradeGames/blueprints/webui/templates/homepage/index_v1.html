Welcome to PyTradeGames!!!

This is a website where people who collect games in physical media can connect, talk and trade games.

It's easy!

Click on the Signup button at the top of the page, then log in with your details.

Now register all the games that you have and would be willing to trade.

img - register
img - add games

Now go to the Users page.

There you can find all the users and their games. You can use the filter on the top of the table to make it easy to find the games you're looking for.

img - users

When you find the user with the rigth game, you just have to click on "Start a Trade" and offer a game that you have.

img - start trade

Use the chat to talk and negotiate the details of the trade, such as delivery and things like that.

img - trade page

When everything is done, the other user must finish the trade, and then, both of you will have to rate the other.

img - rating

And that is it!!!

We hope you have a great experience with us, always with respect and joy. And we are here to help you in every step!

Good Trades!!
